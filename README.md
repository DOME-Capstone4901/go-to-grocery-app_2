Go-To Grocery App, Developed by Team D.O.M.E for Capstone 4901 at the University of North Texas. IOS/Android application geared towards aiding users to shop smarter, reduce food waste, and receive AI-powered reciepe personalizer.

The app helps users track grocery items, set expiry reminders, inventory management and even makes smart suggestions With integrated AI making personalized tips and meal ideas.

Key Features: Smart grocery tracking with expiry alerts AI-powered reminders and suggestions Shopping list planning and management User-friendly dashboard for inventory tracking Cloud-based data synchronization (future phase)

Project Goals: Help households reduce food waste Simplify grocery organization Use technology to make everyday shopping more efficient
